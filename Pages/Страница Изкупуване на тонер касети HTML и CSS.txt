<p>Toner Support Ltd. collects cartridges with proven origin (OEM cartridges*) &mdash; original cartridges from all well-known printer, copier, and MFP** brands on the Bulgarian market: Brother, Canon, Dell, Epson, Hewlett Packard (HP), Lexmark, Ricoh, Samsung, Xerox, Pantum, Okidata, Panasonic, Sharp.</p>

<p>We only collect &quot;virgin&quot; cartridges &mdash; those which were used only once, without being refilled or manipulated.</p>

<p><strong>It is essential to note that Toner Support Ltd. reserves the right to assess the necessity of collecting the offered empty toner cartridges.</strong></p>

<p><strong>*OEM</strong> &ndash; &quot;Original Equipment Manufacturer&quot; &ndash; a term from the industry&#39;s professional language, signifying &quot;a product with proven brand origin that is not counterfeit.&quot;<br />
<strong>** MFP</strong> - Multifunctional Device.</p>

<p><strong>We do not collect the following types of toner cartridges:</strong></p>

<ul>
	<li>
	<p><strong>Compatible Toner Cartridges (&quot;clones&quot;)</strong>: These are low-quality single-use products that may seem attractive due to their low prices but violate patents and pose a threat to the environment due to the substantial amount of industrial waste they generate.</p>
	</li>
	<li>
	<p><strong>&quot;Starter Toner Cartridge&quot;</strong>: This refers to the cartridge with which the manufacturer equipped your brand-new printer or MFP. Starter cartridges differ from standard ones and usually have a lower capacity for fewer copies, often varying in construction.</p>
	</li>
</ul>
