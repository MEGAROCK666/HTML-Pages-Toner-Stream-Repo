Avoid common problems with your laser printer by scheduling preventive or annual maintenance. This includes thorough cleaning and replacement of worn-out parts at regular intervals. Regular servicing can significantly extend the lifespan of your printer and prevent costly repairs.

Signs that your printer needs repair

Do you notice any of these issues?

  • Printed pages are smudged or have lines;
  • Paper frequently jams;
  • Printouts come out wrinkled or dirty.

All these signs indicate that it’s time for laser printer servicing and repair. The fuser unit, together with toner cartridges and the drum unit, is a key consumable of every printer. Timely diagnostics and preventive maintenance can prevent serious damage and ensure smooth operation. Manufacturers recommend at least annual maintenance of the fuser unit to maintain the device’s maximum efficiency.
